It's not about the goal it's about the journey

I believe enjoying the journey is as important as achieving the final goal. I believe pursuing after success, money or fame won't make a person happy in the long-term. A person should enjoy their route in life and evolve through life with what they love to do most. When it comes to me, programming is my passion and this is the path I've chosen. I love programming because I find it challenging and innovative. I love working my mind out and solving a good puzzle. Also, I believe new software comes out to the market all the time which makes the goal endless and this is why we should enjoy our journey.
